About Us

At Tseg Family Child Care, we provide personalized care in a cozy home environment. Our mission is to nurture your child's growth through play, learning, and love. With small group sizes and a family-oriented approach, we ensure every child feels seen and supported.

Our Programs

Infant Care (6 wks – 12 months)

Gentle routines, tummy time, and loving care in a peaceful setting.

Toddler Program (1 – 3 years)

Exploration, early language development, and age-appropriate play.

Preschool Prep (3 – 5 years)

Early learning through stories, art, social play, and structured routines.

After-School (5 – 10 years)

Homework help, crafts, and fun in a safe and supportive space.
